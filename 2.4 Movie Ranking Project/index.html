The Best Movies According to Denis

My top movies of all-times.

Gladiator

Commodus takes over power and demotes Maximus, one of the preferred generals of his father, Emperor Marcus Aurelius. As a result, Maximus is relegated to fighting till death as a gladiator.

Troy

After Paris, a Trojan prince, has an affair with Menelaus's wife, Helen, he decides to take her with him. Later, Menelaus's brother uses that as an excuse to wage a war against the city of Troy.

Shutter Island

Teddy Daniels and Chuck Aule, two US marshals, are sent to an asylum on a remote island in order to investigate the disappearance of a patient, where Teddy uncovers a shocking truth about the place.

Inception

Cobb steals information from his targets by entering their dreams. Saito offers to wipe clean Cobb's criminal history as payment for performing an inception on his sick competitor's son.
